Hello Everyone

Hello, My name is Daniel Gasperini. I am a father, veteran, and computer science major. I have a little girl named Rowan who is one year(s) old who makes my day by dancing and giving me big hugs. I was a Army Ranger and served in Afghanistan. My time in the service taught me how hard I can push myself and I try to still follow the Ranger Creed every day. Lastly, I am a computer science major. The reason I chose computer science was because of my IT work while serving, my jobs ranged from being a radio operator to network administrator for my company commander. after I finished my service, I work overseas for a year in two locations. First was Thule, Greenland for six months and then Diego Garcia, British Indian Ocean Territory for another 7 months. I worked with a section of the Air Force (now Space Force) to track and connect to satellites and rockets. After that job I came home and decided instead of making good money (tax free), I wanted to be a poor college student and start a family. I originally wanted to go to University of Houston however after I experienced how unfriendly and unsupportive, they were, I applied and to UHD and they were much better to deal with. I now am a full-time student, living off the government and scholarships. I am planning to finish school and work for a little bit but mainly start a business. Remember, a job maybe secure but you will always have a ceiling.
